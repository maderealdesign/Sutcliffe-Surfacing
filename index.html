Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a resin bound driveway last?

When installed correctly by City & Guilds qualified professionals onto a suitable sub-base, a high-quality resin bound driveway can last upwards of 15 to 25 years. They are incredibly durable and resistant to cracking.

Do you do interior resin or just driveways?

We specialize in both! Alongside our premium exterior resin bound driveways, we install bespoke indoor epoxy resin floors. These are perfect for modern home interiors, offering a seamless, highly durable, and stunning finish.

Do I need planning permission for a resin driveway?

No. Resin bound surfacing is highly permeable (porous), meaning water drains naturally through it. Because it complies with SUDS (Sustainable Urban Drainage Systems) regulations, you do not typically need planning permission to install it.

Do you handle the groundwork and landscaping too?

Yes, we handle the entire project from start to finish. Our team is fully equipped to manage all aspects of groundworks including flagging, block paving, tarmac, structural concreting, fencing, and general landscaping to ensure a perfect result.
